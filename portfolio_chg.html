About The Project

Club House Group is a collection of like minded underground music producers, engineers, vocalists, designers etc that share a deep love for music in all its various facets, to shine a light on the various artists, and creators in the industry that ultimately shape the culture but do not necessarily get the proper recognition for doing so.
